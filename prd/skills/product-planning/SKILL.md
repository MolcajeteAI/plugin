# Product Planning Skill

## When to Use

- Creating initial product vision and mission
- Planning feature roadmaps
- Making technology stack decisions
- Evaluating strategic alignment of features

## Strategic Interview Questions

### Vision Questions
- What problem does this product solve?
- Why does this problem matter?
- What does success look like?

### User Questions
- Who will use this product?
- What are their key pain points?
- How do they currently solve this problem?

### Differentiation Questions
- What makes this product unique?
- Who are the competitors?
- What's your unfair advantage?

### Roadmap Questions
- What are the must-have features?
- What's nice to have but not critical?
- What's the natural feature progression?

### Tech Stack Questions
- What are your technical requirements?
- What's your team's expertise?
- What are your scaling needs?
- What are your integration requirements?

## Strategic Alignment Checks

When reviewing features:
1. Does this align with product mission?
2. Does this serve target users?
3. Does this leverage our differentiators?
4. Does this fit our tech stack?
